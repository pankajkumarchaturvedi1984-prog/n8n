GTM STRATEGY REPORT

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Indian ice cream market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 16.03% from 2026-2034, driven by rising disposable incomes and rapid urbanization. The impulse ice cream segment dominates the market, and there is a growing demand for premium and artisanal frozen desserts. A comprehensive GTM strategy should focus on targeting the impulse ice cream segment, developing premium and artisanal products, partnering with foodservice channels and online platforms, developing innovative flavors, and investing in effective marketing and advertising campaigns.
