GTM STRATEGY REPORT

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Indian soap market is witnessing a shift towards organic, natural, and eco-friendly products, driven by increasing consumer awareness about personal hygiene and the environment. The growing middle-class population in India is driving growth in the soap market, with higher income levels leading to increased investment in premium and branded soap products. To capitalize on this trend, a comprehensive go-to-market strategy is recommended, focusing on product positioning, pricing, distribution, and promotional activities.

MARKET ANALYSIS
Market Size: The Indian soap market is expected to grow, driven by increasing awareness about personal hygiene, expanding middle-class population, and shifting consumer preferences.
Target Segments: high population growth states, women aged 15-25
Key Trends: increasing awareness about personal hygiene, shift towards organic and natural products, growing middle-class population
